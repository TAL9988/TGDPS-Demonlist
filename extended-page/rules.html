The Extreme Demon Slayers Challenge list Rules

Here are the rules for getting your level/record accepted on the list.


Records

  • Records show that you have completed a level on the list.
  • Your record should be sent through either YouTube, Twitch or Google Drive.
  • dont hack!

    • Submitting Your Level to the List

  • Rules
  • 1. No spam challenges/awkward inconsistent spam
  • 2. Level must have some form of structuring
  • 3. Level must have decent effort put into it
  • 4. The Levels Publisher, Creator and Verifier must all be Active Members of the EDS
  • 5. Level can't be over 45 seconds long
  • 6. Level must be atleast 3 seconds long
  • 7. Level has to be original (cant steal gameplay from other levels) INSPIRATION IS ALLOWED
  • 8. Level cant be absolute trash to play
  • 9. No duplicated gp levels (levels with a piece of gp duplicated multiple times, for example: 3sh in hell)
  • 10. Level cant have any skips/secret ways

    • Eligibility

  • Any record run must begin at the start of the level and continue until the end of the level. A record is still valid even if you get 0 deaths and 100% accuracy in Noclip Accuracy or just use one attempt in Practice Mode.
  • It is necessary that there are audible clicks present throughout the entire run, whether the footage is finished or raw. A record without clicks will definitely be disregarded! mobile players are exempt from this rule for easier levels on the list.


    • Bannable Punishments

  • Show Hitboxes On Death IS allowed. Other than that its just common sense, dont use things that are commonly considered hacks, Noclip, Hitboxes, etc.

    • All credit for the inspiration for these rules goes to the Pointercrate Guidelines.
